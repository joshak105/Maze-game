Optional level description.

The file format consists of
    1st) 5 rows of characters, each row exactly 9 characters. Default tile guide below.
    2nd) The text "player start"
    3rd) A pair of numbers separted by a comma that indicates the initial location the player
    4th) The text "crate starts"
    5th) A list of pairs of numbers (same format as the player start), one per line, that indicates
        the initial positions of the crates.
    6th) The text "goals"
    7th) A list of pairs of numbers (same format as the player start), one per line, that indicates
        the target/goal locations of the crates to complete the map
        The goal list MUST be equal in length to the crate list.
    8th) All text after the goals list is ignored from loading and can be used as comments or for map
        descriptions.
